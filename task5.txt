1. Event Finalization:  Automatically update a sports event's status to "Ended" once all scheduled matches for that specific event are completed.
2. Medal Allocation (Per Event):
Points-Based Tournaments: Upon event completion, automatically award Gold, Silver, and Bronze medals to the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place teams, respectively, based on the final points table.

Knockout Tournaments: Upon event completion, award a Gold medal to the final winner and a Silver medal to the runner-up. 

3. Overall Master Leaderboard:
Create a master points table that aggregates the data across all sports.
For each team, display their total accumulated count of Gold, Silver, and Bronze medals.

4. Sorting/Ranking Logic:
Sort the master leaderboard strictly according the following:
Primary Sort: Highest number of Gold medals.
Secondary Sort: If Gold is tied, highest number of Silver medals.
Tertiary Sort: If Silver is also tied, highest number of Bronze medals.
